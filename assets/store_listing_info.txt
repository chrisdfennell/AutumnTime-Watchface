Autumn — Golden-Hour Fall Foliage Watch Face

(Alternates: "Autumn — Fall Foliage Digital Watch Face" /
 "Autumn: Maple Grove Watch Face")


------------------------------------------------------------------------
FIELD: Description (Max 4000 characters)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Autumn is a premium, high-fidelity digital watch face featuring a warm, crisp golden-hour fall aesthetic. Experience a living sky, drifting clouds, rolling hills, and a swaying grove of fiery maple trees, all procedurally rendered — with leaves that flutter down out of the canopies. Large, outlined digital time and clean complications keep you connected and styled for the season.

FEATURES & DETAILS
• LIVING SKY GRADIENT: A procedural backdrop that shifts colors dynamically by time of day (dawn purples, crisp midday blue, amber/crimson sunset, and a starry dusky-purple night).
• ARCING SUN & MOON: Day/night orbital progression of a glowing harvest sun (with rotating rays and bloom) and a pale crescent moon.
• PROCEDURAL ANIMATIONS: Drifting clouds, gently rolling autumn hills, and a swaying grove of deciduous trees with fiery red/orange/gold canopies.
• FALLING LEAVES: Maple leaves spill out of each tree's canopy and flutter to the leaf-litter forest floor.
• ROTATING SECONDS: A black-outlined maple leaf second indicator floating and orbiting the perimeter, legible even over the ground.
• DYNAMIC COMPLICATIONS:
  - Heart Icon (maple-orange, 16px with black outline) + Body Battery percentage.
  - Water Droplet Icon (golden-amber, 12px with black outline) + device battery percentage.
  - Steps Bar (harvest-gold / burnt-orange) + numeric steps count.
• HIGH-CONTRAST OUTLINES: All texts (clock, date, weather, and battery metrics) feature a crisp black outline for absolute readability against the dynamic visual backdrops.

BUILT FOR YOUR DISPLAY
Autumn adapts to your panel:
• AMOLED (Fenix 8 / tactix 8 / quatix 8 and Fenix 8 Pro): smooth gradient resource fills, specular highlights, and active background illumination.
• MIP / Solar (tactix 8 Solar, Fenix 8 Solar): high-contrast flat fills and optimized backdrops tuned for maximum sunlight readability.
Everything is laid out relative to your screen, so it looks right on every supported case size.

ALWAYS-ON FRIENDLY
On AMOLED, the always-on display switches to a burn-in-safe variant — dim grey time/date, thin outline representations of the battery metrics, steps progress outline, and no visual fills or background animations. All lit pixels are shifted a few pixels each minute. MIP panels always show the full face.

SETTINGS
• Show Date — toggle the date line.
• Step Goal Override — steps for a full harvest bar; 0 uses the watch's own step goal.

PRIVACY
Autumn uses only the SensorHistory permission to read Body Battery. It does not collect or transmit any data off your watch.

SUPPORTED DEVICES
tactix 8 (AMOLED & Solar), Fenix 8 (43/47/51mm AMOLED), Fenix 8 Solar, Fenix 8 Pro, quatix 8, Forerunner 965.

Equip Autumn, and take a moment in the crisp fall air.
