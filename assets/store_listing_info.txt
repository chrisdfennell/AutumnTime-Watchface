Autumn — Golden-Hour Fall Foliage Watch Face

(Alternates: "Autumn — Fall Foliage Digital Watch Face" /
 "Autumn: Maple Grove Watch Face")


------------------------------------------------------------------------
FIELD: Description (Max 4000 characters)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Autumn is a premium, high-fidelity digital watch face featuring a warm, crisp golden-hour fall aesthetic. Experience a living sky, drifting clouds, rolling hills, and a swaying grove of fiery maple trees, all procedurally rendered — with leaves that flutter down out of the canopies. The sky and sun track your real sunrise and sunset, and two configurable complications keep the data you care about on your wrist. Large, outlined digital time keeps you connected and styled for the season.

FEATURES & DETAILS
• LIVING SKY ON REAL SUN TIMES: A procedural backdrop that shifts colors dynamically by time of day (dawn purples, crisp midday blue, amber/crimson sunset, and a starry dusky-purple night), anchored to the actual sunrise/sunset for your location and date.
• ARCING SUN & MOON: Day/night orbital progression of a glowing harvest sun (with rotating rays and bloom) and a pale crescent moon, following the true day arc.
• PROCEDURAL ANIMATIONS: Drifting clouds, gently rolling autumn hills, and a swaying grove of deciduous trees with fiery red/orange/gold canopies.
• FALLING LEAVES: Maple leaves spill out of each tree's canopy and flutter to the leaf-litter forest floor.
• ROTATING SECONDS: A black-outlined maple leaf second indicator orbits the perimeter, drawn on top of everything else so it stays legible over the time, date, and complications.
• CONFIGURABLE COMPLICATIONS: Choose what each bottom slot shows — Heart Rate, Body Battery, Device Battery, Steps, or Calories (or turn it Off) — each with a matching warm-toned icon. Defaults: bottom-left = Heart Rate, bottom-right = Device Battery. A harvest-gold steps bar with a live count sits below.
• HIGH-CONTRAST OUTLINES: All texts (clock, date, weather, and complication values) feature a crisp black outline for absolute readability against the dynamic visual backdrops.

BUILT FOR YOUR DISPLAY
Autumn adapts to your panel:
• AMOLED: smooth gradient resource fills, specular highlights, and active background illumination.
• MIP / Solar: high-contrast flat fills and optimized backdrops tuned for maximum sunlight readability.
Everything is laid out relative to your screen, so it looks right on every supported case size.

ALWAYS-ON FRIENDLY
On AMOLED, the always-on display switches to a burn-in-safe variant — dim grey time/date, thin outline representations of the complications, steps progress outline, and no visual fills or background animations. All lit pixels are shifted a few pixels each minute. MIP panels always show the full face.

SETTINGS
• Show Date — toggle the date line.
• Step Goal Override — steps for a full harvest bar; 0 uses the watch's own step goal.
• Bottom-Left Complication — Heart Rate, Body Battery, Device Battery, Steps, Calories, or Off.
• Bottom-Right Complication — same options (default: Device Battery).

PRIVACY
Autumn uses only the SensorHistory permission to read Body Battery. It does not collect or transmit any data off your watch.

SUPPORTED DEVICES
Every Connect IQ 4.0+ round Garmin watch — Forerunner (165, 255, 265, 570, 955, 965, 970), fenix 7/8 (incl. Solar) & fenix E, epix 2 / Pro, enduro 3, Venu 2/3 (& Venu 4), Vivoactive 5/6, Instinct 3 / E / Crossover, plus Approach (golf), Descent (dive), D2 (aviation), and MARQ specialty watches. Square/rectangular panels (Venu Sq 2, Venu X1) are not supported.

Equip Autumn, and take a moment in the crisp fall air.
